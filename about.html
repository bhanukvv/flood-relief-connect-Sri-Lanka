About Flood Relief Connect

Connecting communities in times of need

üéØ Our Mission

Flood Relief Connect was created to bridge the gap between those affected by floods and those willing to help. We provide a simple, real-time platform where victims can request assistance, donors can offer help, and collection centers can coordinate relief efforts.

Our goal is to make disaster relief more efficient, transparent, and accessible to everyone in Sri Lanka who needs it or wants to contribute.

üìç Understanding the Map Pins

üî¥ Need Help

Red pins indicate people who urgently need assistance during floods. Each pin shows their location, contact details, and what specific help they require. Click on any red pin to see how you can help.

üü¢ Want to Help

Green pins represent generous donors and volunteers offering assistance. They show what resources, services, or support they can provide. Connect with them to coordinate relief efforts.

üîµ Collection Centers

Blue pins mark official collection centers where you can drop off donations. Each center lists what items they're accepting and provides contact information for coordination.
